Dittman Construction Company Ltd is a fully Kenyan-owned business that specializes in constructing roads, bridges, and various civil engineering projects. Registered in Kenya, the company's main office is situated in Eldoret at Vomorono House on the 1st floor. Additionally, it has a branch in Nairobi called Dittman Centre, located near the KPA Inland Container Depot along Mombasa Road.
